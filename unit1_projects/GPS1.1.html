Why pair program?

Quality of work will increase by sharing ideas amongst an engineering team and within DBC
Helps build a stronger team, with more efficient and useful communication.
Learning from one another due to diverse backgrounds/experiences
People probably have more satisfaction for their jobs by working with a team vs. programming purely on their own
Leads to disagreement to refine ideas and come to a consensus
Sharing a common goal creates a more efficient work environment.
Two eyes are always better than one -- useful for debugging.
